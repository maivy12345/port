About

I design complex digital products by turning user and business challenges into clear, scalable experiences.

With 3+ years across healthcare, B2B, eCommerce, and service platforms, I specialize in multi-role systems, conversion-critical journeys, and end-to-end product design grounded in real user behavior, operational logic, and measurable outcomes.

What Defines My Work

I approach design as a series of product cases — each one rooted in a real problem, shaped by real constraints, and measured by real outcomes.

I work closely with product, business, and engineering teams to ensure solutions are not only visually polished, but also practical, measurable, and ready to scale.

My strengths include:

  • Simplifying complex user flows
  • Structuring information for clarity and action
  • Designing for multiple user roles and touchpoints
  • Improving conversion through UX audits and flow optimization
  • Building reusable systems that reduce design and development friction
